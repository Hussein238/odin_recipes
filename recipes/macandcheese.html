Macaroni And Cheese Recipe

What is it?

A dish of Bri'ish origin made up of cooked macaroni pasta and a cheese sauce, most commonly Cheddar sauce.
In north america the dish is a popular comfort food because of its simple preparition and high caloric level.

    Ingredients

  • 8 ounce elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • ground black pepper

    Steps

  1. Add macaroni into a pot of boiling lightly salted water, stir occasionally for 8 minutes.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Add flour, salt, and pepper into the saucepan and stir for about 5 minutes.
  4. Pour in milk, while stirring continuously for about 5 minutes.
  5. Add cheddar cheese and stir until melted, 3 minutes
  6. Drain macaroni, then pour cheese sauce and mix.
