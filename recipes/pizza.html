Simple Pizza Recipe

A Simple And Easy To Make Homemade Pizza!

    Ingredients

  • 1 cup of warm water
  • 0.25 ounce active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of white sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups of bread flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

    Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 230 degrees C, then lightly grease a pizza pan
  2. Add warm water, yeast and sugar in a bowl. Mix until creamy; about 10 minutes
  3. Add flour, oil and salt to the yeast mixture; beat the mixture until smooth either by hand or use a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook. Let rest for 5 minutes.
  4. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle.
  5. Move the dough to the pizza pan then spread sauce and toppings of your choice on top.
  6. Bake in the oven until golden brown, 15-20 minutes then remove and let cool for 5 minutes.
