LASAGNA!

What Is This!

The 14th century was a time when everyone was depressed and/or dying from the Black Plague which killed all their friends and family, to cheer up everyone in Italy they invented Lasagna.

Ingredients

    Meat sauce

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • Onion
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of diced onion
  • 28 ounce tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces tomato paste
  • pinch of garlic powder or garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • pinch of salt

    • Pasta

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounce ricotta cheese
  • 24 ounce mozzarella cheese

    Steps

    Sauce

  1. Put a pot of water on to boil with 1 tablespoon of salt for every 2 quarts of water
  2. Pour 2 teaspoons of olive oil in a skillet on medium to high heat then add the ground beef and cook until it's lightly brown all over.
  3. Turn the heat to low then add the diced onion to the skillet and stir for 5 minutes or until the onions are translucent
  4. transfer everything in the skillet into a pot then add the rest of the ingredients for the meat sauce and stir together
  5. Bring sauce to a simmer then lower the heat and cook for 30 minutes

    6. Noodles

  6. Add the lasagna noodles to the pot of water and cook, per package instructions
  7. Stir often to prevent noodles sticking then once ready drain in a colander and rinse woth cool water, separating any noodles that stuck together
  8. Spread olive oil on a rimmed baking sheet and lay out the cooked noodles then turn them over so they are coated with olive oil
  9. preheat oven to 190*C
  10. In a 9x13-inch casserole dish spread the sauce over the bottom of the dish, then arrange a layer of noodles over the sauce
  11. spread a third of the sauce over the noodles; sprinkle a third of the mozzarella on the sauce and half of the ricotta cheese.
  12. Add a second layer of noodles and top it with half of the remaining sauce then half of the mozzarella and all the ricota cheese left.
  13. Finish with a final layer of noodles and spread the rest of your sauce, and cheese

Bake:

Cover the lasagna with aluminum foil, and bake at 190*C for 30-40 minutes; uncover for the last 10 minutes if you'd like a more crusty top. Lastly allow the lasagna to cool for at least 15 minutes before serving.
